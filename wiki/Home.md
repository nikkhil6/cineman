## What is Cineman?

Cineman is an intelligent movie recommendation agent that combines:
- **Google Gemini AI** for natural language understanding and recommendations
- **TMDB API** for movie posters and metadata
- **OMDb API** for ratings and additional movie information
- **Flask web interface** for easy interaction
